Three 6 Mafia


"You done seen the guage pump bout to see a Chevy trunk"


The most known, yet unknown, dirty south rap group from the streets of Memphis. They came together in 1991 and lit up the scene through the 2000's. A short revival of the group occured in the 2010's but it was short lived. Three 6 Mafia has won the Academy Award (2006), they have two platnimum albums (2000 and 2005), and they have sold over 5 million albums worldwide. Rest in Peace Lord Infamous and Koopsta Knicca.
